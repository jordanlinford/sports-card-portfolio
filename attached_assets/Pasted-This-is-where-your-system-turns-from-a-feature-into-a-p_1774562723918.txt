2. What the “Market Leaderboard” Actually Is

Not a popularity list.
Not just top prices.

It is:

A ranked view of player markets based on real-time opportunity, strength, and risk.

3. Core Leaderboards You Should Build (Start with 3)
3.1 “Best Markets Right Now” (Primary)

This is your hero surface.

Ranking logic:
rank_score =
  (market_score * 0.5) +
  (momentum_score * 0.2) +
  (demand_score * 0.2) +
  (confidence_score * 0.1)
Output:
Rank	Player	Score	Phase	Verdict	Key Signal
1	Player A	82	Breakout	ACCUMULATE	Demand +22
2	Player B	79	Accumulation	ACCUMULATE	Supply tightening
3	Player C	76	Expansion	HOLD	Stable liquidity
3.2 “Hype / Sell Candidates”

This becomes your most viral feature.

Ranking logic:
hype_rank =
  (hype_score * 0.5) +
  (momentum_score * 0.3) -
  (volume_trend * 20)
Output:
Rank	Player	Hype Score	Phase	Signal
1	Player X	88	Exhaustion	Price > volume
2	Player Y	84	Expansion	weak participation
3	Player Z	81	Exhaustion	rising supply
3.3 “Emerging Opportunities”

This is where you create alpha.

Ranking logic:
opportunity_score =
  (demand_score * 0.3) +
  (volume_trend * 30) +
  (low_hype_bonus) +
  (early_phase_bonus)

Where:

low_hype_bonus = (100 - hype_score) * 0.2
early_phase_bonus = 15 if phase in ["Accumulation", "Breakout"] else 0
Output:
Rank	Player	Phase	Why
1	Player D	Accumulation	volume rising, price stable
2	Player E	Breakout	demand spike + low hype
3	Player F	Accumulation	supply tightening
