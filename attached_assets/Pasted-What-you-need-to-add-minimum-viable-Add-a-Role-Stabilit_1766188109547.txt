You need a role signal.

Given your constraints (scraper barely passable), the simplest workable approach is:

Create a small, editable dictionary in your app (or admin UI) that assigns players to a role tier:

UNCERTAIN_STARTER: 45

BACKUP: 25

Then incorporate roleStabilityScore into downsideRiskScore:
downsideRiskScore = max(existingDownsideRiskScore, 100 - roleStabilityScore)

→ AVOID_NEW_MONEY

Pickett, Mac Jones, Ridder, Lance, Wiseman-type

Option B: Lightweight automated heuristics (still no scraping)
If you really want less manual work, you can infer roleTier from very basic metadata you likely have:

team present or not

current team depth chart status (you probably don’t have)

whether the player appears in “starter” lists (needs web; not recommended for runtime)

Realistically, Option A is the dependable approach for MVP.
