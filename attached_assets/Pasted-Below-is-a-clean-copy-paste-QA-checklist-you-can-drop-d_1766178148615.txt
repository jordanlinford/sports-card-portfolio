Global Engine Guardrails (Hard Rules)

Check after every run:

⬜ No ROOKIE has HOLD_CORE or MEDIUM/HIGH confidence
⬜ No modeled comp returns MEDIUM or HIGH confidence
⬜ SPECULATIVE_FLYER appears meaningfully (not zero, not everything)
⬜ AVOID_NEW_MONEY is reserved for structural negatives
⬜ HOLD_CORE dominates proven stars and retired legends

When to Stop Tuning

You are done tuning when:

These tests pass twice in a row

Changes in thresholds move only 1–2 players, not entire categories

You can explain every verdict in one sentence without hedging

At that point, lock the engine and move on to UX, copy, and trust-building.
