PHASE 1 — THE "WOW" FEATURES (Fast, Viral, Low Engineering)
Goal: Get collectors sharing instantly. Growth without paid ads.

Collectors share only when it looks beautiful.

Add quickly:

3 layout styles (grid, row, angled card spread)

Hover animations for stats

Optional title + subtitle (e.g., "My QB Grails")

Why:
This is uniquely YOUR space. No competitor does this.
Shareable visuals = instant reach.

Minimal engineering — just make sure your OG image generation is always clean and consistent.
