Runs on schedule.

System 3: Alpha Engine (new)

Generates signals from snapshots

Table: card_signals
Field	Type
card_id	string
alpha_score	float
signal_type	enum (buy/sell/hold)
confidence	string
reasoning	text
created_at	timestamp
Simple v1 scoring (keep this lightweight)
undervaluation = (current_price - avg_price_7d) / avg_price_7d
momentum = trend_pct_7d
liquidity = observation_count
confidence = function(observation_count, variance)

alpha_score =
  undervaluation_weight +
  momentum_weight +
  liquidity_weight

No ML. No complexity.
