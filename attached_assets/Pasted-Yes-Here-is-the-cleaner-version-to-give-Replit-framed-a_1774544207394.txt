Add a market phase layer

This is important because it gives the engine better sports-card-native logic than just hot/warm/cooling.

Market phase	What it means
Accumulation	Quiet market, stable prices, demand starting to build
Breakout	Price and activity rising together
Expansion	Strong demand, broad participation, prices elevated
Exhaustion	Prices still high but participation weakening
Decline	Prices falling, supply rising, demand fading
