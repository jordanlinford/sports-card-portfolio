Notes (important for correctness)

This Part 3 is intentionally verdict-shaping, not “perfect roster truth.”
It’s designed to stop the exact misclassifications you were seeing:

Pickett / Ridder / Lance mistakenly HOLD/ACCUMULATE

Bust QBs not going AVOID

Retired icons drifting into speculative/avoid

A few of these players (e.g., Rodgers/Kelce) are still active/late-career; they belong in FRANCHISE_CORE for purposes of market behavior and collector demand, even if on-field context changes.

If you want the registry to reflect exact 2025 team situations, you can add a separate field later (team, status), but don’t let that complicate the verdict engine yet.
