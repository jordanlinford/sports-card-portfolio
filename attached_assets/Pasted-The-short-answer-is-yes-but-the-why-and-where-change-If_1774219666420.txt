• The Verdict: You still need Scraper Resilience (Proxies/VPS) for your pop_history engine because Google Search Grounding won't give you the granular, recurring tabular data you need to build that moat.

• The Risk: These sites use Cloudflare Turnstile or JavaScript Challenges to stop bots.
• The Verdict: To get the high-fidelity "Solds" that a "Whale" collector expects, you still need a scraper that can execute JavaScript and mimic a real browser session.
3. The "Grounding Limit" (Cost & Quotas)
• The Reality: Gemini’s Google Search Grounding is powerful but has a 1 million query per day limit (and costs per request).  
• The Strategic Play: For high-volume tasks like Batch Card Analysis (#47) where a user might upload 20-50 cards at once, using a custom scraper for the "heavy lifting" (raw data) and Gemini for the "reasoning" (investment verdict) is often 5x cheaper and faster than grounding every single card query.
