How the Homepage Should Change (Now That Market Outlook Exists)

Your homepage should pivot from:

“Look at your cards beautifully”

To:

“Know what your cards are worth — and where they’re going.”

Display cases are still important, but Market Outlook becomes the hero.

⸻

A. Above-the-Fold (This Is the Most Important Change)

Current problem (likely):
	•	Too abstract
	•	Too collector-focused
	•	Value not immediately measurable

New Above-the-Fold Structure:

Headline

See where your card’s value is headed — before you buy, sell, or grade

Subheadline

Real sales data + AI market signals.
Free users get 3 outlooks/month. Pro users get unlimited.

Primary CTA
	•	🔍 Run a Free Market Outlook

Secondary CTA
	•	💎 Upgrade for Unlimited Outlooks

This immediately:
	•	Anchors value in money
	•	Sets up the free → paid conversion
	•	Makes the tool feel expensive (but accessible)

⸻

B. Immediate Proof Section (Right Under the Hero)

You need to show the output, not explain it.

Section Title

What a Market Outlook Shows You

4-column visual or cards
	•	📈 Price trend (30 / 90 / 180 days)
	•	🔄 Volatility & liquidity
	•	🧠 AI outlook (Bullish / Neutral / Risky)
	•	💡 Action suggestion (Hold / Buy / Grade / Sell)

Key line underneath

Based on real sales — not guesses or hype.

This builds trust fast.

⸻

C. Freemium Framing (This Is Where Conversion Happens)

You should explicitly explain the limit, but position it as generous.

Section Title

Try It Free. Upgrade When It Pays for Itself.

Two columns

Free
	•	✅ 3 Market Outlooks per month
	•	✅ Real sales comps
	•	✅ Basic AI outlook
	•	❌ Limited history depth
	•	❌ No saved outlooks

Pro
	•	🚀 Unlimited Market Outlooks
	•	📊 Deeper historical trends
	•	💾 Save & track cards over time
	•	🧠 Advanced AI signals
	•	🖼️ Full Display Case features

CTA
	•	“Unlock Unlimited Market Outlooks”

This makes Pro feel inevitable for anyone serious.

⸻

D. Reposition Display Cases (Important Shift)

Display Cases should move down the page and be reframed as a benefit of insight.

Instead of:

“Show off your collection”

Use:

“Show off collections backed by data.”

Tie it directly to Market Outlook:
	•	Cards in display cases show:
	•	Last outlook status
	•	Trend arrows
	•	Confidence badge (High / Medium / Speculative)

This keeps Display Cases relevant without leading with them.

⸻

E. Homepage Flow (TL;DR)
	1.	Hero: Market Outlook value
	2.	Proof: What insights you get
	3.	Freemium: 3 free vs unlimited
	4.	Use cases: Buy smarter / Sell smarter / Grade smarter
	5.	Display Cases: Social + data-backed
	6.	Final CTA: Run your first outlook
