Reframing the Problem (First Principles)

A low-print card only matters if:

There are enough buyers competing for that scarcity.

So the true equation is:

Component	Role
Player demand	Sets the ceiling and competition
Liquidity	Determines how often price discovery happens
Rarity	Multiplies scarcity within that demand
Product tier (NT, Prizm, etc.)	Sets baseline desirability

Right now, your system is over-weighting rarity and under-weighting demand.

That’s why:

Clingan /8 → inflated
Hurts /5 → probably correct
Vick /5 → likely overstated
